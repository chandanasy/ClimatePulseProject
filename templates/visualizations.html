Climate Pulse - Visualizations


About Climate Pulse Visualizations

We are using sentiment analysis to gain valuable insights into public opinion and attitudes towards different US government and presidential terms in the context of climate change social media data. By analyzing text data related to every US government and presidential term, we aim to understand how sentiment changes over time.

Analyzing Sentiments by US Government and Presidential Terms

Through our analysis of social media data related to every US government and presidential term, we are uncovering patterns and trends in public opinion towards climate change policies and decisions made during that period. By tracking changes in sentiment over time, we aim to gain insights into how public perception of the government's actions on climate change evolves.
